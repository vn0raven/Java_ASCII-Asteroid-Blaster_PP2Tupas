# Combat Balance Pass Notes

This update responds to playtest feedback after directional shooting and asteroid shrapnel were added.

## Main fixes

1. Directional shooting was adjusted so left, right, and diagonal shots feel more centered.
2. Shot Level 1 now alternates its side bullet instead of always favoring one side.
3. Shot Level 2 now fires a balanced three-way fan around the direction the ship is facing.
4. Asteroids were made tankier to balance the stronger directional and multi-shot systems.
5. Meteor shard clusters now appear more often and can form larger jagged patterns.
6. Shrapnel bursts were increased so asteroid destruction feels more chaotic and satisfying.
7. The danger limit was tightened to make missed asteroids matter more.

## Updated asteroid durability

- Heavy asteroid: 5 hits
- Standard asteroid: 3 hits
- Light asteroid: 2 hits

## Updated shrapnel burst counts

- Heavy asteroid: 9 shards
- Standard asteroid: 6 shards
- Light asteroid: 4 shards

## Updated progression feel

The game is now balanced around the idea that directional shooting makes aiming easier, so the enemy field has been made denser and asteroids take more commitment to destroy.
